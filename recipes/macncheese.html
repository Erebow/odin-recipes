Mac and Cheese!

  • Macaroni
  • 2 types of Cheeses
  • A semi-large pot
  • Water

Steps

  1. Measure out the needed amount of macaroni
  2. Put the macaroni as well as water half the amount of the pot in the pot
  3. Let the macaroni come to a simmer and, while stirring, add cheese
  4. Continue stirring until the macaroni is mushy and cheesy
