Pizza

A rounded dough cooked with cheese, tomato sauce, pepperoni's and like seasoning. The crust comes out nice and crunchy, with little air bubbles.

  • Dough
  • Tomato Paste
  • Pepperoni
  • Garlic seasoning
  • Cheese of your choice

Steps

  1. Roll out dough into a nice even circle with slightly raised edges.
  2. Evenly spread the tomato paste across the surface of the dough, excluding the raised portion.
  3. Sprinkle cheese to lightly cover most of the surface of the dough.
  4. Lastly, add pepperoni across the dough, adding as much as you would like.
  5. Enjoy!
