Peanut Butter and Jelly!

  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly
  • Bread of your choice
  • Spreading knife

A delicious sandwhich that has peanut butter on one side and jelly on the other, combined in order to create a classic nostalgic dish.

Steps

  1. Lay out 2 pieces of bread
  2. Spread peanut butter on one slice and jelly on the other
  3. Put the pieces of bread together
  4. Optional - Cut in half with the knife
  5. Enjoy!
